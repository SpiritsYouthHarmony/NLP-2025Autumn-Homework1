Voices from CIFIT: How do foreign enterprises do business in China? - People's Daily Online The 25th China International Fair for Investment & Trade (CIFIT) takes place in Xiamen from September 8 to 11. As one of the world's most influential international investment events, it attracts government officials and multinational companies from around 100 countries and regions.
