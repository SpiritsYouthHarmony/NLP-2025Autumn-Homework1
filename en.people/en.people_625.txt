Panda cub feasts on Mid-Autumn mooncakes - People's Daily Online This Monday, giant panda cub Mei Zhu celebrated her second Mid-Autumn Festival at Guangzhou Chimelong Safari Park. The female cub enjoyed special mooncakes made with panda-friendly ingredients, along with fresh carrots and bamboo shoots prepared by her keepers.
