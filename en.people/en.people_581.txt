Wuhan in October: Witness the harvest of China-Nordic economic cooperation - People's Daily Online Under the theme of "Smarter Synergies for a Stronger Pillar," the 2025 China-Nordic Economic and Trade Cooperation Forum is being held in Wuhan, central China's Hubei Province from Oct. 15 to 16. Organized by the Ministry of Commerce and the Hubei Provincial People's Government, the Forum is China's first and currently only long-term mechanism dedicated to economic and trade cooperation with Nordic countries, aiming to become an important platform for Hubei Province to build a highland for China's inland opening-up and a flagship initiative promoting openness in central China. This year, representatives from over 30 countries and regions, eight provinces and municipalities across China, more than 200 Fortune Global 500 companies, and over 2,000 entrepreneurs are attending the Forum, as it provides them with a precious platform to discuss potential cooperation opportunities and to exchange thoughts and ideas.
