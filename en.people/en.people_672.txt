Australian diplomat: Leaders from both countries show strong support for CIFTIS - People's Daily Online On Wednesday, Dominic Trindade, Commercial Minister at the Australian Embassy in Beijing, said Chinese President Xi Jinping's congratulatory letter to the 2025 China International Fair for Trade in Services (CIFTIS), and Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese's address to the Global Trade in Services Summit of CIFTIS reflect strong backing from both governments at the highest level, adding that businesses in both countries will welcome this support.
