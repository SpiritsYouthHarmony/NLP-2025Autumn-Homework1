China Everything Vlog | Exploring the fusion of tradition and trend in Beijing's White Pagoda - People's Daily Online The historic White Pagoda Temple area in Beijing's Xicheng District is undergoing a renaissance. Once a tranquil and traditional neighborhood, it now buzzes with trendy cafes, restaurants, and other stores. In July, the local government launched three themed routes in this area focusing on history, photography, and food. Join People's Daily intern Xiong Yitong and explore the pagoda and immerse yourself in the fresh energy of this historic neighborhood.
