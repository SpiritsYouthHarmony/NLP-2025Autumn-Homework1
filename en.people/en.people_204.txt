China's Zhang Qingying wins balance beam title at Artistic Gymnastics World Championships - People's Daily Online Zhang Qingying of China celebrates winning the women's balance beam final at 53rd FIG Artistic Gymnastics World Championships in Jakarta, Indonesia, on Oct. 25, 2025. (Xinhua/Bai Xuefei) JAKARTA, Oct. 25 (Xinhua) -- China's Zhang Qingying won the women's balance beam title on 15.166 points at the 53rd FIG Artistic Gymnastics World Championships here on Saturday.
