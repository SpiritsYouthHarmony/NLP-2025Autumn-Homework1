Global mayors explore China's 'porcelain capital' - People's Daily Online (Photo/People's Daily Online) From Oct. 17 to 20, the "2025 Global Mayors Dialogue · Jingdezhen" was held in Jingdezhen city in east China's Jiangxi Province. Mayors and city representatives from countries including Italy and Türkiye, along with guests from the cultural sector, explored Jingdezhen's museums, alleyways, and intangible cultural heritage workshops, immersing themselves in the unique charm of China's "porcelain capital."
