China's Zou Jingyuan wins parallel bars title at Artistic Gymnastics World Championships - People's Daily Online Zou Jingyuan of China celebrates during the the Men's Parallel Bars Final at 53rd FIG Artistic Gymnastics World Championships in Jakarta, Indonesia, on Oct. 25, 2025. (Xinhua/Bai Xuefei) JAKARTA, Oct. 25 (Xinhua) -- China's Zou Jingyuan won the men's parallel bars title at the 53rd FIG Artistic Gymnastics World Championships here on Saturday. Zou clinched the gold with a score of 15.300 points.
