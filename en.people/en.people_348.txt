View of Dongpo Academy in Danzhou, China's Hainan - People's Daily Online This photo taken on Oct. 24, 2025 shows a view of the Dongpo Academy in Danzhou, south China's Hainan Province. The Dongpo Academy was once a place where Su Dongpo gave lessons in ancient times. Su Dongpo, also known as Su Shi, is a poet and cultural icon from the Song Dynasty (960-1279), who was demoted in his later years and lived in exile in south China's island province of Hainan for three years.
