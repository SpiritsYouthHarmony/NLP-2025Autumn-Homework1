Spectacular monorail ride from the driver's perspective - People's Daily Online Get ready for a fantastic journey through the mountainous megacity of Chongqing. This video captures the view just before entering Liziba Station on Chongqing Rail Transit Line 2, a 31-km-long monorail with 25 stations. Liziba Station is uniquely built into a residential building, making it a popular attraction. (Edited by Huang Jingjing and Ma Ziyuan)
