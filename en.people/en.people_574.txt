China's rocket launches Pakistan remote-sensing satellite into orbit - People's Daily Online China on Sunday launched the Lijian-1 Y8 carrier rocket with three satellites onboard. The rocket blasted off at 11:33 am (Beijing Time) from a commercial aerospace innovation pilot zone in Northwest China and successfully sent the satellites into the planned orbit. The three satellites are Pakistan remote-sensing satellite (PRSS-2), AIRSAT 03 and 04 satellites.
