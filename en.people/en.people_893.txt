People pay tribute to Chen Ning Yang at Tsinghua University in Beijing - People's Daily Online People pay tribute to Chen Ning Yang at Tsinghua University in Beijing, capital of China, Oct. 19, 2025. Renowned physicist Chen Ning Yang, a Nobel laureate and academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, died in Beijing on Saturday at 103, according to Tsinghua University.
