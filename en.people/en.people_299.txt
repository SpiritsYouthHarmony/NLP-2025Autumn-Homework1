Over 1.9 mln people across Gaza Strip displaced during Gaza war: UN - People's Daily Online Palestinians gather at a street vendor under the destroyed buildings in Jabalia, northern Gaza Strip, Oct. 24, 2025. The United Nations estimates that approximately 92 percent of all residential buildings in Gaza -- around 436,000 homes -- have been damaged or destroyed since the start of the conflict. According to the UN, at least 1.9 million people across the Gaza Strip have been displaced during the war.
