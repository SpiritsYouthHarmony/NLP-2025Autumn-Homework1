Trending in China | Pingyang Huangtang Yellow Tea - People's Daily Online Pingyang Huangtang Yellow Tea is a prized tea from Zhejiang Province in East China. The leaves are finely shaped and have a bright apricot color, delivering a sweet and refreshing taste. This tea is celebrated for its "three yellows": the yellow dried leaves, the apricot-yellow tea liquor and the tender yellow brewed leaves. Like a wise hermit among teas, it offers comfort and is truly worth savoring. (Produced by Kang Yutong and Wu Shuyi; Video source: Kuaishou)
