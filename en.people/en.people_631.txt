Trending in China | Slow-wheel pottery: The Dai people's ancient craft - People's Daily Online For thousands of years, the Dai people of Southwest China's Yunnan Province have made everyday essentials out of clay with a traditional method deeply rooted in their culture. Using distinctive slow pottery wheels, artisans can craft an endless variety of pieces with intricate textures and delicate patterns. This time-honored craft still thrives in villages across the southwestern border region of Xishuangbanna. (Video source: Kuaishou)
