Lily Unpacks | Inside the world's first robot & AI museum in Seoul - People's Daily Online The Seoul Robot & AI Museum (RAIM), the world's first of its kind, offers an interactive experience that connects global innovators and showcases the future of technology. People's Daily reporter He Jieqiong visited RAIM and spoke with guests from the 2025 China-South Korea Media Cooperation Forum about the evolving relationship between humans, robots and AI, as well as the potential for China-South Korea collaboration in this field.
