Foreign visitors captivated by Yunnan's ethnic minority dances - People's Daily Online Foreign visitors from Latin America were drawn to Yunnan's ethnic dances, featuring vibrant rhythms, at the 2025 Creative Yunnan Culture Industries Expo in Kunming, Yunnan Province, on Wednesday, September 17. The expo, which runs until Sunday, highlights Yunnan's cultural heritage and the growth of its cultural industries. (Produced by Xie Runjia, Zhang Wujun and Han Ruxin)
