(Xinhua/Wang Fei) BEIJING, Oct. 24 (Xinhua) -- China aims to raise the average life expectancy of its people to around 80 years through efforts in the five-year period from 2026 to 2030, said the country's top health official on Friday. The current life expectancy of Chinese is 79 years, said Lei Haichao, head of the National Health Commission.
