Highland Creatures: Where are the snow leopards - People's Daily Online Did you know that 60 percent of the world's snow leopards live in China, with most of them on the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau? Mysterious, alert and beautifully camouflaged, it takes sharp eyes and a bit of luck to spot them. That's why infrared cameras are the go-to tool in wildlife conservation to observe and protect these snowy mountain kings.
