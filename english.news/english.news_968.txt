An enduring source of China-Russia friendship: a bond forged with blood-Xinhua 80 years ago, the Chinese people and the Russian people jointly made indelible historical contributions to the victory in the World Anti-Fascist War and forged an unbreakable great friendship with blood. Xinhuanet spoke with Boris Cheltsov, scientific secretary of the Victory Museum in Russia, to hear his recount of the profound friendship forged with blood and lives by the two peoples as they fought side by side.
