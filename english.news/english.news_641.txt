GLOBALink | Boxers exchange more than punches in Xinjiang-Xinhua The 2025 Belt and Road International Youth Boxing Exchange Tournament opened Monday in Urumqi, capital of China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. More than 400 boxers, coaches and staff from 20 countries and regions, including China, Cuba, Kazakhstan, India and South Korea, are taking part following a week of joint training.
