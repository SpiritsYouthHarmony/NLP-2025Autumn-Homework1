Chinese yuan strengthens to 7.0856 against USD Tuesday-Xinhua BEIJING, Oct. 28 (Xinhua) -- The central parity rate of the Chinese currency renminbi, or the yuan, strengthened 25 pips to 7.0856 against the U.S. dollar Tuesday, according to the China Foreign Exchange Trade System. In China's spot foreign exchange market, the yuan is allowed to rise or fall by 2 percent from the central parity rate each trading day.
