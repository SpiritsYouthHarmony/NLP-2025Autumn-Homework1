62nd anniversary of Evacuation Day marked in Bizerte, Tunisia-Xinhua Tunisian President Kais Saied attends a ceremony marking the 62nd anniversary of Evacuation Day at the Martyrs' Cemetery in Bizerte, Tunisia on Oct. 15, 2025. Evacuation Day marks the anniversary of the departure of the last French soldier from Tunisia on Oct. 15, 1963. (Tunisian Presidency/Handout via Xinhua)
