First phase of 138th Canton Fair concludes with broad global participation-Xinhua Buyers negotiate with exhibitors at the 138th edition of the China Import and Export Fair in Guangzhou, south China's Guangdong Province, Oct. 15, 2025. The first phase of the 138th edition of the China Import and Export Fair, also known as the Canton Fair, concluded on Sunday. According to statistics from China Foreign Trade Center, about 157,900 overseas buyers from 222 countries and regions attended the event offline.
