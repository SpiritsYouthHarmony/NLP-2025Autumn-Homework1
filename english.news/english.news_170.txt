6.1-magnitude quake hits Türkiye -- GFZ-Xinhua BEIJING, Oct. 28 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 6.1 jolted Türkiye at 1948 GMT on Monday, the GFZ German Research Centre for Geosciences said. The epicenter, with a depth of 10.0 km, was initially determined to be at 39.18 degrees north latitude and 28.20 degrees east longitude. ■
