Yunding Snow Park to host FIS World Cup events this winter-Xinhua Yunding Snow Park in north China's Hebei Province, a 2022 Winter Olympic venue, will host a series of international competitions this winter, including the FIS Freeski and Snowboard Big Air World Cup, the Snow League, the FIS Freeski and Snowboard Halfpipe World Cup, and the FIS Freestyle Ski World Cup.
