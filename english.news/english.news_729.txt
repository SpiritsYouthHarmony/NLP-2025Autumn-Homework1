China urges U.S. to stop harassing Chinese students-Xinhua BEIJING, Aug. 22 (Xinhua) -- China calls on the U.S. side to thoroughly investigate and correct its errors of unwarrantedly questioning, harassing and repatriating Chinese students upon their entry into the United States, Chinese foreign ministry spokesperson Mao Ning said on Friday.
