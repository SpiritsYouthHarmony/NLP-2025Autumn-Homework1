Urgent: 5.9-magnitude quake hits Türkiye -- GFZ-Xinhua CAIRO, Oct. 27 (Xinhua) -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 5.9 jolted Türkiye at 19:48:30 GMT on Monday, the GFZ German Research Center for Geosciences said. The epicenter, with a depth of 10 km, was initially determined to be at 39.18 degrees north latitude and 28.20 degrees east longitude. ■
