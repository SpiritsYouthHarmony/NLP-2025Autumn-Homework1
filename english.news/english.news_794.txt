Global Digital Economy Conference | Former UN Under-Secretary-General praises China's indispensable role in green economy-Xinhua "Digital economy can help poor nations develop faster towards a greener future." Speaking at the 2025 Global Digital Economy Conference, Erik Solheim, Former Under-Secretary-General of the United Nations, emphasized that China's achievements in renewable energy and environmental protection are unmatched globally, making it an essential player in accelerating the global green transition.
