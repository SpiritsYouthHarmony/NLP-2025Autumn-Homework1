"Sculpture by the Sea" exhibition held at seaside in Sydney, Australia-Xinhua Visitors view exhibits at the seaside in Sydney, Australia, Oct. 24, 2025. Featuring sculptures by artists from Australia and across the world, the outdoor exhibition dubbed "Sculpture by the Sea" is held along the Bondi to Tamarama coastal walk in Sydney and will run until Nov. 3 this year.
