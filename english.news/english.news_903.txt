Chinese Culture Festival held at Badr University in Cairo-Xinhua A student tries to make Chinese paper-cutting during a Chinese cultural event held at Badr University in Cairo, Cairo Governorate, Egypt, on Oct. 20, 2025. The Badr University in Cairo on Monday held a Chinese Culture Festival at its campus, with exhibition stands of Chinese calligraphy, paper-cutting and handicrafts, as well as activities including Chinese martial arts performance and ethnic costumes show, attracting lots of students and teachers.
