Local train club offers model trains for visitors to have ride in South Africa-Xinhua Visitors ride on a model train operated by a train enthusiast at the Centurion Society of Model Engineers, Centurion of Gauteng, South Africa, Oct. 19, 2025. The Centurion Society of Model Engineers, a well-known local train club, offered a variety of steam locomotives and other model trains for visitors to have a ride.
