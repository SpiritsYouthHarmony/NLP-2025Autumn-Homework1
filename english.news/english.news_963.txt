China Trends | Global South solidarity for gender equality: Cambodian minister-Xinhua The Global Leaders' Meeting on Women, scheduled to be held in Beijing from October 13 to 14, has drawn wide attention from the international community. “The Global Leaders' Meeting on Women is not only a celebration of the 30 years of what we have done, but it's also a renewed momentum to renew our commitment,” said Ing Kantha Phavi, Cambodian Minister of Women’s Affairs, in an exclusive interview with Xinhua ahead of the meeting. She noted that China’s progress in promoting women’s empowerment is admirable. Cambodia hopes to strengthen cooperation with China to jointly advance women’s development and looks forward to South-South cooperation creating broader opportunities for women around the world.
