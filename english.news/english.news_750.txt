GLOBALink | Jingdezhen and Iznik's Ceramic Dialogue on the Silk Road-Xinhua Türkiye and China recently inaugurated the first official Jingdezhen porcelain flagship store in Istanbul, marking a step forward in cultural and economic partnership between the Chinese city and Türkiye's historic ceramic hub, Iznik. The artistic dialogue, initiated centuries ago, continues to inspire modern partnerships. Embark on an enchanting journey with Xinhua correspondent Du Baiyu, to explore the cross-cultural dialogue between the two historic ceramic centers. The deal builds on a sister-city partnership established in 2021 and formalizes efforts to promote joint exhibitions, research, and trade initiatives.
