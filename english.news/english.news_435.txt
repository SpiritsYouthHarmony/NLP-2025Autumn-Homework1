3 killed in bus rollover in south China-Xinhua NANNING, Oct. 28 (Xinhua) -- Three people were killed in a bus rollover in south China's Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, local authorities said on Tuesday. The accident occurred at about 3:20 p.m. in Sishui Village, which is located in Longsheng County of Guilin City. All remaining passengers have been rescued and those who sustained injuries have been sent to hospital, according to the county's emergency management bureau.
