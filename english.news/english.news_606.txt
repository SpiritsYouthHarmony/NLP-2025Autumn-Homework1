24th Lao Handicraft Festival held in Vientiane-Xinhua A stall owner displays handicrafts during the 24th Lao Handicraft Festival in Vientiane, Laos, Oct. 23, 2025. The festival is held here from Oct. 22 to 26. More than 130 exhibition booths have been set up, displaying textiles, jewellery, pottery, wood carvings, etc. In addition, visitors can enjoy a wide range of interactive cultural experiences during the festival.
