Marigolds harvested ahead of Day of the Dead in Mexico City-Xinhua Farmers harvest marigold flowers in Mexico City, capital of Mexico, Oct. 21, 2025. Every year on Nov. 1 and 2, Mexicans celebrate the Day of the Dead. In the days leading up to the festival, fields of marigolds are harvested and brought to markets, eventually adorning streets, homes, and cemeteries across the country.
